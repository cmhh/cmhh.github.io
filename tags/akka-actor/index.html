Agent Based Simulations with Akka

Published at January 9, 2022 ·  16 min read

Overview In this post we describe a relatively simple agent-based simulation. The simulation itself is a rough approximation of a household survey, consisting of a large collection of dwellings, in turn surveyed in a strictly face-to-face setting by one of a number of independent field collectors. We describe the simulation in terms of the actor model, though we do so in a way that is largely agnostic with respect to programming language or actor framework....
